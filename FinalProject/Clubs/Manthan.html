Manthan Club

VISION STATEMENT

The Manthan Club at NMIMS, Navi Mumbai strives to engage and inspire diverse passionate students by providing them a platform to showcase their ideas with skills and simultaneously adapting to the latest tools and technologies, thus making projects that will give them, as well as the world a future vision.

MISSION STATEMENT

Organizing sessions related to the project topics so that students have a better idea and knowledge regarding the assignments they will work on and improve the skills needed to establish values that will help to act with thoughtfulness.

OBJECTIVES

  • Guide and provide assistance wherever needed to students so that they are able to complete their projects in the time frame.
  • To make projects compatible with the ongoing trends in the technology and participate in the main as well as other colleges fests and competitions.
  • Hold exhibitions to showcase the projects.
