Techcider Club

The vision of our club is to provide a dynamic extracurricular platform for individuals eager to expand their interests and refine their skills using the latest technologies. We aim to offer structured opportunities for students to explore diverse interests while leveraging cutting-edge tools and technologies in a professional manner. Our mission is to cater to all levels of tech enthusiasts, from beginners to advanced learners, by organizing interactive sessions, facilitating access to online courses for skill development, and promoting peer-to-peer learning through completion of certificate programs. Through guest lectures, workshops, coding competitions, and knowledge dissemination about emerging trends, we strive to foster a tech-savvy community and empower students for success in internships and future careers.

Infinix Club

The Infinix Coding Club is for everyone who is keen to learn about programming, algorithms, statistics and how these skills can be applied in the real world, real practical applications. We want to create and promote a diverse community where everyone has a voice and feels comfortable to express it. We all have the right to learn, and learning is more fun and efficient when we help each other along the way.
